Welcome to Salad Caffe Admin Panel

This is the Super Admin dashboard for managing:

  • Territories - Create and manage delivery territories
  • Kitchens - Create kitchens under territories
  • Subscription Plans - Create subscription plans with dynamic pricing
  • Menu Schedule - Schedule meals for the month
